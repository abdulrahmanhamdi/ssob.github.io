International Student Services

At Sivas Cumhuriyet University, we are dedicated to supporting our international students throughout their academic journey. Our comprehensive services are designed to help you adapt to your new environment and succeed in your studies. Here are some of the key services we offer:

Doktora Programları

Our dedicated team assists with visa applications and provides guidance on immigration regulations to ensure a smooth process for you.

Lisans Programları

We offer orientation sessions to help you understand the university environment, meet fellow students, and get acquainted with Sivas city.

Türkçe Hazırlık (TÖMER)

We provide language courses and support services to help you improve your academic and conversational English skills.

Yüksek Lisans Programları

Our cultural exchange programs and social events will help you connect with local students and experience Turkish culture.

Yüksekokullar ve Meslek Yüksek Okulları

Our academic advisors are available to help you with course selection, academic planning, and any other academic concerns you may have.
